HCDE 533 - Digital Fabrication

Hello! I'm Sara!

Why digital fabrication? I have a background in software, specifically web dev, but my hands-on experience is unfortunately limited. I've always loved creating things with my hands but I have never really made the jump into machine and computer assisted work.

My artistry background is mostly in textiles. I love fashion design and sewing. However, I am still an amatuer, but am mostly comfortable around sewing machines.

Beyond sewing, I took HCDE 539 Physical Computing & Prototyping with Adi Azulay in fall 2021, which introduced me to soldering and working with microcontrollers. I was really able to flourish due to my technical background--it was always the physics of electronics that was throwing me off! By jumping right in via a prototyping course, I found myself much more comfortable in exploring new things.

That course was part 1 of my adventure into tangible work, and I consider HCDE 533 (this course) to be part 2.

I have also dabbled in laser cutting, glass cutting, and minor woodworking.
